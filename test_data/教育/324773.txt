08年10月自考英语写作常识
　　一、图形种类及概述法
　　泛指一份数据图表：a data graph or chart or diagram or illustration or table
　　饼图：pie chart
　　直方图或柱形图：bar chart or histogram
　　趋势曲线图：line chart or curve diagram
　　表格图：table
　　流程图或过程图：flow chart or sequence diagram
　　程序图：processing or procedures diagram
　　以下是一些较常用的描述用法
　　The table or chart diagram or graph shows (that)
　　According to the table or chart diagram or graph
　　As (is)shown in the table or chart diagram or graph
　　As can be seen from the table or chart or diagram or graph or figures，
　　figures or statistics shows (that)……
　　It can be seen from the figures or statistics
　　We can see from the figures or statistics
　　It is clear from the figures or statistics
　　It is apparent from the figures or statistics
　　table or chart or diagram or graph figures (that)……
　　table or chart or diagram or graph shows or describes or illustrates how
　　二、data的具体表达法
　　data在某一个时间段固定不变：fixed in time
　　在一系列的时间段中转变：changes over time
